# Business KPIs – AI Retail Decision Support System

## 1. Purpose
This document defines the key business performance indicators (KPIs) used to
evaluate the impact of the AI-powered retail decision support system.
The goal is to translate AI outputs into measurable business value.

---

## 2. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### 2.1 Staffing Efficiency
**Description:** Measures how effectively staff are allocated during peak and off-peak hours.

**How it is calculated:**
- Compare customer foot traffic with staffing levels.
- Identify periods of overstaffing and understaffing.

**AI Module Used:**
- Computer Vision (Foot Traffic Analysis)

**Business Value:**
- Reduced labor costs
- Improved customer service during busy hours

---

### 2.2 Sales Growth Rate
**Description:** Measures the percentage increase or decrease in sales over time.

**How it is calculated:**
- Compare current sales to previous periods (daily/weekly).

**AI Module Used:**
- Sales Forecasting (POS Data)

**Business Value:**
- Early detection of declining sales
- Better planning for promotions and inventory

---

### 2.3 Inventory Efficiency
**Description:** Evaluates how well inventory matches customer demand.

**How it is calculated:**
- Compare predicted sales with actual sales.
- Detect overstock and stock-out risks.

**AI Module Used:**
- Sales Forecasting

**Business Value:**
- Reduced cash tied in inventory
- Fewer lost sales due to stock shortages

---

### 2.4 Customer Satisfaction Index
**Description:** Measures overall customer sentiment based on reviews.

**How it is calculated:**
- Aggregate sentiment scores over time.
- Track changes in dominant complaint topics.

**AI Module Used:**
- NLP (Customer Review Analysis)

**Business Value:**
- Improved customer retention
- Faster response to service issues

---

### 2.5 Customer Value Segmentation
**Description:** Categorizes customers based on purchasing behavior.

**How it is calculated:**
- Clustering based on Recency, Frequency, and Monetary value.

**AI Module Used:**
- Customer Segmentation

**Business Value:**
- Targeted marketing
- More effective promotions

---

### 2.6 Decision Response Time
**Description:** Measures how quickly business owners can obtain insights.

**How it is calculated:**
- Time required to access insights via chatbot vs manual analysis.

**AI Module Used:**
- AI Chatbot

**Business Value:**
- Faster decision-making
- Reduced management overhead

---

## 3. KPI–AI Module Mapping

| KPI | AI Module |
|---|---|
| Staffing Efficiency | Computer Vision |
| Sales Growth Rate | Sales Forecasting |
| Inventory Efficiency | Sales Forecasting |
| Customer Satisfaction | NLP |
| Customer Segmentation | Clustering |
| Decision Response Time | Chatbot |

---

## 4. Business Impact Summary
The system enables retail owners to:
- Reduce operational costs
- Increase revenue
- Improve customer satisfaction
- Make faster and more informed decisions

---

## 5. Limitations
- KPIs are estimated using historical or simulated data.
- Results depend on data quality and availability.
- The system is designed as a decision-support tool, not a fully autonomous system.
